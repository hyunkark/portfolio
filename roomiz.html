Roomiz
 Mar 2019 - Jul 2019
Team Project(4 people)
Service Ideation, Prototyping, Graphic Design
 "Your house's butler, right at your fingertips!"

Roomiz will enable your happy shared house life!
Using PEST method, we've connected the three main trends -Non traditional family, Querencia, Shared economy- to come up with an idea, improving current shared house life so that it would last long enough to become an actual family.
We made 3 personas- one for shared house manager, one for shared house tenant, and one for an anti persona- so we could come up with the features that would be absolutely essential for a better shard house life.
We also made Customer Journey Maps for each personas.
The final product is a high fidelity prototype, enabled by protopie.
