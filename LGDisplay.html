LG Display Project
 Dec 2018 - Feb 2019
Team Project (3 people)
Service Ideation, Illustration, Video editing
 A RFP(​Request for proposal) project. The goal was to develop a new product that could be used in the future that can be made with OLED Displays. Made personas and scenarios for the new display product. The initial pitch was a cylinder disaply that can substitite pillars in architecture.
Whilst developing the pillar display, we came across this new idea of sportive display-a display that can be used as a yoga mat and also as a partition screen. The final product was a 2 min video about the usage of the screen.
