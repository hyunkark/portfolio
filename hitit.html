hit it!
 Sep 2019 - Dec 2019
Personal Project (Graduation Project)
Service Ideation, GUI, Prototyping
 Got no time to work out? Don't know how to work out? No more of those excuses.
Let's hit the road with hit it!

'hit it!'is a smartphone app that will help you make use of those public parks exercise machines that you've never known how to use. By selecting workout time and what park you'd like to workout at, hit it will conveniently come up with a full workout schedule tailored for you in a second. You can also catch up with the latest workout events led by state facilities or the government with ease.
The final product is a high fidelity prototype, enabled by protopie.
Also, usable t-shirts and waterbottle, and banners for sports event and posters were designed following the same design system. The GUI and the branded goodies will give an integrated look for sporting events.
